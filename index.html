Engineering Explorations

Experimentation within engineering projects

Wireless power transmission

Through simulations conducted with COMSOL Multiphysics, a wireless charger model was created, incorporating various shielding materials such as copper, aluminum, and air. The study specifically focused on the impact of power inductance at varying distances from the wireless charger. By utilizing MATLAB for data analysis, thresholds and distances at which the electromagnetic fields generated by the wireless charger could pose potential dangers to human health were identified.

Arduino Based Cobot

Our project focused on building an #Arduino-based cobot, controlled via an #ESP32 through a mobile app. This hands-on endeavor provided a practical introduction to cobot programming, delving into the intrecacies of collaborative robot operations. The study extended to evaluating #mechanical limits, ensuring an optimal balance between performance and structural integrity. Additionally, it explored crucial considerations in electrotechnics, defining the boundaries for seamless functionality.

Characterization of 3D printed springs

Due to limited information on Nylon (SLS) 3D printed parts. A plan of experiments was implemented, fabricating 27 springs with unique configurations to experimentally determine their stiffness. This study integrated material science with an emphasis on automation, aiming to identify the spring configuration that would achieve optimal stability.

Development of Digital Twin

The project focused on developing a digital twin for an existing production line, using TwinCAT's GRAFCETs, ladder logic, and structured text programming. A 3D simulation, with visual components, mirrored real-world operations. The digital twin provided an environment for testing.

FM Transmitter

Building a basic FM transmitter was a hands-on project that helped to understand the fundamental concepts of electronics. This practical experience focused on the basics of signal processing, offering a direct exploration of FM signal transmission.
